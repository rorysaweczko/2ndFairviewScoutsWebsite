About Beavers

Beavers is for kids aged 5-7. If you have kids of this age, this is the perfect place for them! According to Scouts Canada, Beavers will help to develop better and well rounded children who are better prepared for success in the world. Through the Plan-Do-Review method of Scouting and the Youth Led style of all sections, leadership and teamwork can be developed. Great Scouting adventures will help Beaver Scouts to share ideas, learn new things, and develop new skills.


Beavers can offer any number of new experiences for your child. Because of the Youth-Led aspect of Scouting, kids can try anything they can imagine. Whether your child likes swimming, hiking, games, movies, or just sitting around at a campground, Beaver Scouts is the perfect place to do this.


Beaver Scouts provides a little bit of everything for young kids. Enroll with 2nd Fairview to help your child grow and learn in many different areas.


Wondering what kind of activities your child can participate in in Beaver Scouts? Check out some of the images to the side, or take a look at our promotional video on the main page! In addition, you can take a look around the website and look at some of the images. This will help to give you a good idea of what you can expect in Beavers.


Still not convinced Beaver Scouts is for you? Why don't you try it out! 2nd Fairview allows 2 free meetings before you have to register for the Scouting year. Come down to our hall on a Thursday night from 6:00 to 7:30 and check out the fun things you can do with Beavers.
