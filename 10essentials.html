The 10 Essentials

  • Navigation: Compass, map, GPS, etc.
  • Light: Preferably a flashlight (NOT a headlamp). Extra batteries are a good idea.
  • Protection from Elements: Sun protection, bug protection, rain protection, etc.
  • First Aid: Simple is fine. Bandages, gauze, etc.
  • Knife. Please be aware that only Scouts who have recieved a knife permit can carry a knife in Scouting.
  • Fire: This includes matches, fire-starters, and sometimes small, dry wood. Please avoid lighters, as the fuel can run out at any time.
  • Shelter: This should be brought in the form of a tarp or emergency blanket.
  • Food: Bring non-perishable, extra food.
  • Water: At least 2 litres, or a water filter.
  • Rope. Hempen rope is good. It can be bought very cheaply at the dollar store.
