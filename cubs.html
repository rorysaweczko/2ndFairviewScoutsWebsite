About Cubs

Cubs is for kids aged 8-10. If you have kids of this age, this is the perfect place for them! According to Scouts Canada, Cubs will help to develop better and well rounded children who are better prepared for success in the world. Through the Plan-Do-Review method of Scouting and the Youth Led style of all sections, leadership and teamwork can be developed. Great Scouting adventures will help Cub Scouts to share ideas, learn new things, and develop new skills.


Cubs offers something for every kind of child. The youth-lead aspect of Scouting allows the kids to plan adventures and other activities that would suit them. Whether your child likes walks outdoors, winter camps, snowball fights, laser tag, exploring museum, or just hanging out around a campfire, Cub Scouts is the perfect place to combine all of these activities.


Wondering what kind of activities your child can participate in in Cub Scouts? Check out some of the images to the side, or take a look at our promotional video on the main page! If you don't see anything that interests you there, don't be discouraged. Cub Scouts allows for limitless different opportunities; there is always something that will interest you.


Wondering what kind of camps your child can participate in? Check out our Camping Opportunites page for more information! In addition, you can take a look around the website at some of the pictures scattered around. These will help to give you a good idea of what to expect.


Still not convinced? Come for free! 2nd Fairview allows 2 meetings to try out Cubs and see if you like it before registering for the year. Cubs meet from 6:00 to 8:00 on Wednesday evenings. Why not come down to the hall and check it out? We would love to see you!


If you have any other questions about Cub Scouts, feel free to take a look at the FAQs on the "How to Join" page. If you are interested in become a Scouter with your child, check out the volunteering page, or click on this link! We hope to see you and your child in Cub Scouts soon!
